**DO (On-Brand):**

* "Here's how to maximize your 401(k) contributions for 2025..."
* "Tax-loss harvesting can help you reduce your taxable income—here's how it works..."
* "Let's break down the difference between traditional and Roth IRAs..."

**DON'T (Off-Brand):**

* "Get RICH QUICK with this ONE investment trick!"
* "The stock market is about to CRASH—here's what you MUST do NOW!"

### Content Objectives

* **Objective**: Educate individuals on evidence-based personal finance and investing strategies; help readers build wealth through low-cost index investing and tax optimization; promote financial independence
* **Primary KPI**: Newsletter subscriptions; retirement calculator downloads; financial planning template downloads; time-on-page for comprehensive guides; affiliate conversions (brokerage accounts)

### Content Formats

* **Formats**: Step-by-step financial planning guides, investment strategy explanations, tax optimization tutorials, comparative analyses (Roth vs Traditional), case studies, calculator/spreadsheet tools
* **Content Mix**: Planning guides (45%), Investment education (30%), Tax strategies (20%), News/updates (5%)
* **Depth**: Beginner to intermediate; occasional advanced tax/estate planning topics
* **Length**: Guides: 1,500-2,500 words; Tax strategies: 1,200-2,000 words; Comparisons: 1,000-1,800 words; News: 600-1,000 words

  * Index Fund Investing and Portfolio Construction
  * Tax Optimization Strategies

  * Estate Planning Basics

### SEO & Distribution

* **SEO Intent**: Target "401k contribution limits 2025", "Roth IRA vs Traditional IRA", "tax-loss harvesting", "index fund investing for beginners", "how to retire early"; build topic clusters around retirement accounts, tax strategies, and FIRE
* **Internal Linking**: Link planning guides to relevant calculators; connect tax strategy articles to retirement planning; cross-link related account types (401k → IRA → HSA)

* **Review Workflow**: Draft → Financial accuracy review (CPA/CFP consultation) → Legal/compliance check (disclaimers) → Editorial → Publish
* **Cadence**: 2 posts/week (1 evergreen guide + 1 timely update/news)

## ADDITIONAL NOTES

### Target Audience Insights & Pain Points

* Overwhelmed by financial jargon and complex investment products
* Uncertain about 401(k) vs. IRA, Roth vs. Traditional decisions
* High earners paying too much in taxes, unaware of optimization strategies
* Fear of investing due to market volatility—need reassurance and long-term perspective
* Confused by conflicting financial advice from social media/friends/advisors
* Want to retire early but don't know how to calculate FI number or withdrawal strategies

### Editorial Guardrails

* ALL content must include disclaimer: "This is educational content, not personalized financial advice. Consult a qualified financial advisor or tax professional for your specific situation."
* Avoid specific stock recommendations or market timing predictions
* Cite sources for all statistics, tax rules, and contribution limits (link to IRS/SEC official pages)
* Disclose affiliate relationships clearly and transparently
* Do not guarantee investment returns or promise specific outcomes
* Note when tax rules are complex and professional consultation is recommended
* Update tax-related articles annually when IRS rules/limits change
